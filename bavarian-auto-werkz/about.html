The Bavarian Auto Werkz Story

Founded on expertise, built on trust, and driven by genuine passion for BMW excellence.

Our Beginning

Bavarian Auto Werkz was founded in 2009 with a simple mission: provide BMW owners with expert service they can trust. What started as a passion project has grown into the premier BMW specialist shop in our community.

Family Values

We're a family business in every sense of the word. Every customer becomes part of our extended family, and we treat each BMW that enters our shop with the same care and attention we'd give our own vehicles.

Expertise & Growth

Over 15 years later, we've grown from a small garage to a full-service BMW specialist facility. Our team continues to expand their knowledge and certifications to stay current with BMW's latest technology.

Certifications & Credentials

Professional certifications and ongoing training ensure your BMW receives factory-level care.

BMW Factory Certified

Our technicians hold current BMW certifications and regularly update their training to stay current with new models, systems, and diagnostic procedures.

Advanced Diagnostics

We use professional BMW diagnostic equipment and software, giving us the same diagnostic capabilities as BMW dealerships for accurate problem identification.

European Specialist

While BMW is our specialty, we also service other European luxury brands including Audi, Mercedes-Benz, and Porsche with the same level of expertise and attention to detail.

Our Service Philosophy

The principles that guide everything we do for you and your BMW.

Honest Communication

We believe in transparent, straightforward communication. We'll explain what we find, what we recommend, and why - always giving you the information you need to make the best decisions for your BMW.

Quality First

We never cut corners. Every service is performed to BMW's exact specifications using genuine parts or BMW-approved alternatives. Your BMW's performance and reliability are never compromised.

Personal Relationships

We're not a corporate service center. You'll work with the same team members who get to know you, your BMW, and your driving needs. This personal approach allows us to provide better, more tailored service.

Continuous Improvement

We're always learning and improving. From new diagnostic techniques to the latest BMW technologies, we invest in training and equipment to ensure we can properly service any BMW that comes through our doors.
